> 「**出淤泥而不染，濯清涟而不妖。**」
>
> "Risen from the silty mud, yet untainted; washed in the clear ripples, yet not coquettish."
>
> — Zhōu Dūnyí (周敦颐, Zhōu Dūnyí), «On the Love of the Lotus» (《爱莲说》)

Lotus seed (莲子, liánzǐ) is **the dried, mature seed of the lotus (Nelumbo nucifera, family Nelumbonaceae)**.
Harvested when **the lotus is in full summer bloom**, it is **shelled and the seed core (莲心, liánxīn) removed**,
then sun-dried.

> 「**莲子甘平养心神，补肾固精健脾胃。**」 ("Sweet and neutral, the lotus seed nourishes Heart and spirit, tonifies the Kidneys and secures essence, fortifies the Spleen and Stomach.")

Lotus seed is a fine **dual-use food and medicine**:
as medicine, it **nourishes the Heart and quiets the spirit, fortifies the Spleen and stops diarrhea, tonifies the Kidneys and secures essence**;
in cuisine, **lotus seed congee, white-fungus-and-lotus-seed soup, lotus seed and lily bulb soup**.

In the height of summer, **a bowl of lotus seed soup** is **most clearing to the Heart**.

## I. Nature, Flavor, and Channel Entry of Lotus Seed

- **Nature and flavor:** **sweet, astringent, neutral**
- **Channels entered:** **Spleen, Kidney, Heart**
- **Effects:** **tonifies the Spleen and stops diarrhea, benefits the Kidneys and astringes the essence, nourishes the Heart and quiets the spirit**

> 「**莲子，补脾涩肠、固精止带、养心安神，**」
> 「**乃脾肾心三经之要药。**」
> ("Lotus seed: tonifies the Spleen and astringes the intestines, secures essence and stops vaginal discharge, nourishes the Heart and quiets the spirit — it is a key herb for the three channels of Spleen, Kidney, and Heart.")
>
> — *Běncǎo Gāngmù* (《本草纲目》, Compendium of Materia Medica)

## II. Three Major Effects of Lotus Seed

### 1. Nourishes the Heart and Quiets the Spirit

> 「**心者，君主之官，神明出焉。**」 ("The Heart is the office of the sovereign; spirit-light arises from it.")

Lotus seed **enters the Heart channel** and can **nourish the Heart and quiet the spirit, benefit wisdom and settle the will**.
It is effective against **palpitations, insomnia, profuse dreams, and forgetfulness**.

- **Lotus seed core (莲子心)** — the **green-blue plumule** in the center — strongest for **clearing Heart fire (清心火, qīng xīn huǒ)**
- **Lotus seed flesh (莲子肉)** — the **white part** enclosing the core — strongest for **nourishing the Heart and quieting the spirit**

> 「**夏日养心，莲子第一。**」 ("For nourishing the Heart in summer, lotus seed is foremost.")

#### Suitable People

- Those with insomnia and profuse dreams
- Those with palpitations and irregular heartbeat
- Those with declining memory
- Prevention of senile dementia
- Those with emotional anxiety

#### Classic Formulas

- **Lotus Seed Core Tea (莲子心茶):** **3 g of lotus seed core + 3 g of licorice (甘草, gāncǎo)** — **clears the Heart and descends fire**
- **Lily Bulb and Lotus Seed Soup (百合莲子汤):** **30 g of lily bulb (百合, bǎihé) + 30 g of lotus seed + rock sugar** — nourishes the Heart and quiets the spirit
- **Tiān Wáng Bǔ Xīn Dān (天王补心丹, Heavenly King's Heart-Tonifying Pill)** — contains lotus seed; treats Heart-Kidney non-communication, insomnia, and forgetfulness

### 2. Fortifies the Spleen and Stops Diarrhea

> 「**脾为后天之本。**」 ("The Spleen is the root of postnatal life.")

Lotus seed **enters the Spleen channel**; it **fortifies the Spleen and boosts qi, astringes the intestines and stops diarrhea**.
Effective against **Spleen-deficient chronic diarrhea, poor appetite, and indigestion**.

#### Suitable People

- Those with Spleen-deficient chronic diarrhea
- Those with poor appetite
- Those with indigestion
- Those with chronic colitis
- Children with infantile malnutrition (gān jī, 疳积)

#### Classic Formulas

- **Lotus Seed and Chinese Yam Congee (莲子山药粥):** **30 g lotus seed + 50 g shānyào (山药) + 80 g polished rice (粳米, jīngmǐ)** — fortifies the Spleen and stops diarrhea
- **Shēn Líng Báizhú Sǎn (参苓白术散, Ginseng-Poria-Atractylodes Powder)** — contains lotus seed; treats Spleen deficiency with damp exuberance
- **Lotus Seed and Euryale Seed Congee (莲子芡实粥):** **30 g lotus seed + 20 g qiànshí (芡实) + 80 g polished rice** — double tonifies Spleen and Kidney

### 3. Tonifies the Kidneys and Secures Essence

> 「**肾藏精，为先天之本。**」 ("The Kidneys store essence and are the root of prenatal life.")

Lotus seed **enters the Kidney channel**; it **benefits the Kidneys and secures essence, restrains urination and stops vaginal discharge**.
Effective against **seminal emission, spermatorrhea, leukorrhea, and enuresis**.

#### Suitable People

- Men with seminal emission and spermatorrhea
- Women with excessive leukorrhea
- Children with enuresis
- Those with frequent nocturia
- Those with Kidney-deficient low back pain

#### Classic Formulas

- **Jīn Suǒ Gù Jīng Wán (金锁固精丸, Golden Lock Essence-Securing Pill)** — contains lotus seed; treats seminal emission and spermatorrhea
- **Lotus Seed and Euryale Seed Soup (莲子芡实汤):** **30 g lotus seed + 20 g qiànshí** — secures essence and stops vaginal discharge
- **Lotus Seed Stewed with Black-Bone Chicken (莲子炖乌鸡):** **50 g lotus seed + black-bone chicken** — tonifies the Kidneys and secures essence

## III. How to Select Lotus Seeds

### 1. By Place of Origin

- **Xiāngtán, Hunan** — «**Xiāng lotus**», the **authentic-source region (道地, dàodì)**
- **Guǎngchāng, Jiangxi** — «**Gàn lotus**», mostly **white lotus**
- **Jiànníng, Fujian** — «**Jiàn lotus**», mostly **red lotus**
- **Hubei** — «**Hú lotus**», **large yield**

**Xiāng lotus** and **Jiàn lotus** are the **authentic-source materia medica**; **Xiāng lotus** is the finest.

### 2. By Color

- **White lotus** — peeled and decored, **white with a faint yellow**
- **Red lotus** — with skin and core, **brown-red**
- **Polished lotus** — surface smooth, **pure white**
- **Sulfur-fumigated lotus** — overly white, **with a sulfur smell** (**not to be bought**)

### 3. By Appearance

- **High grade:** plump kernels, natural color, firm texture
- **Low grade:** shriveled kernels, dull color, musty smell

### 4. By the Seed Core

- **High grade:** **emerald-green core** (strongest in clearing Heart fire)
- **Decored** — the flesh tends toward **tonifying**
- **With core** — stronger in **clearing Heart fire**

### 5. Simple Tests

- **Pinch:** high-grade lotus seeds are **firm and do not crumble**
- **Smell:** high-grade ones have **a fresh fragrance, no off-odor**
- **Soak:** high-grade ones **sink** and **stay intact** after soaking

## IV. Everyday Lotus-Seed Food Therapy

### 1. White Fungus and Lotus Seed Soup (**Beautifies and Quiets the Spirit**)

- **Method:** 1 piece of white fungus (银耳, yín'ěr, Tremella) + 30 g lotus seed + 5 jujubes (大枣, dàzǎo) + rock sugar
- **Effects:** **enriches yin and moistens dryness, nourishes the Heart and quiets the spirit, beautifies and combats aging**
- **For:** old and young alike, especially women

### 2. Lotus Seed and Lily Bulb Congee (**Nourishes the Heart and Quiets the Spirit**)

- **Method:** 30 g lotus seed + 30 g lily bulb + 80 g polished rice + honey
- **Effects:** **clears the Heart and quiets the spirit, moistens the Lung and stops cough**
- **For:** those with vexation and insomnia, dry cough with scant phlegm

### 3. Lotus Seed and Chinese Yam Congee (**Fortifies the Spleen and Stops Diarrhea**)

- **Method:** 30 g lotus seed + 50 g shānyào + 80 g polished rice
- **Effects:** **fortifies the Spleen and boosts qi, stops diarrhea**
- **For:** those with Spleen-deficient chronic diarrhea

### 4. Lotus Seed and Euryale Seed Soup (**Double Tonifies Spleen and Kidney**)

- **Method:** 30 g lotus seed + 20 g qiànshí + 10 g fúlíng (茯苓)
- **Effects:** **tonifies the Kidneys and secures essence, fortifies the Spleen and stops diarrhea**
- **For:** those with low-back and knee soreness, chronic diarrhea, vaginal discharge

### 5. Lotus Seed Core Tea (**Clears the Heart and Descends Fire**)

- **Method:** 3 g lotus seed core + 3 g licorice, **steeped in boiling water**
- **Effects:** **clears Heart fire, lowers blood pressure**
- **For:** Heart-fire exuberance, mouth and tongue sores, hypertension

### 6. Lotus Seed Stewed with Black-Bone Chicken (**Tonifies Kidneys and Nourishes Blood**)

- **Method:** 50 g lotus seed + half a black-bone chicken + 5 jujubes + 3 slices of ginger
- **Effects:** **tonifies the Kidneys and nourishes blood, fortifies the Spleen and nourishes the Heart**
- **For:** post-illness, postpartum, post-surgical recovery

### 7. Lotus Seed and Chìxiǎodòu Congee (**Fortifies the Spleen and Dispels Dampness**)

- **Method:** 30 g lotus seed + 30 g chìxiǎodòu (赤小豆) + 10 g qiànshí + 80 g polished rice
- **Effects:** **fortifies the Spleen and dispels dampness, nourishes the Heart and quiets the spirit**
- **For:** heavy dampness with Spleen deficiency, Heart and spirit unsettled

### 8. Lotus Seed and Longan Congee (**Nourishes Blood and Quiets the Spirit**)

- **Method:** 30 g lotus seed + 20 g longan (桂圆, guìyuán) + 80 g polished rice
- **Effects:** **nourishes blood and quiets the spirit, tonifies and benefits Heart and Spleen**
- **For:** those with insufficient Heart blood, insomnia, forgetfulness

## V. Dietary Contraindications for Lotus Seed

### ❌ 1. Those with Constipation Should Eat Little

Lotus seed is **astringent**; for those with constipation, it can **worsen the condition**.
> 「**收涩之品，肠燥者忌。**」 ("Astringent items are contraindicated in intestinal dryness.")

### ❌ 2. Those with Damp-Filled Middle Should Eat Little

- Abdominal distension, greasy tongue coating
- Phlegm-damp, damp-heat exuberance

### ❌ 3. Caution at the Onset of an External Contraction

In the acute stage of cold, fever, or sore throat, **first expel the evil, then tonify**.

### ❌ 4. Diabetics Should Eat Little

Lotus seed contains **considerable starch**; **moderate the intake**.

### ❌ 5. Lotus Seed Core Is Cold; Those with Stomach Cold Should Limit It

- **Lotus seed core** — cold in nature, **clears Heart fire**
- **Those with stomach cold or diarrhea** — drink lotus seed core tea less
- **Ordinary people** — **decored** is gentler

## VI. The Lotus Seed Core — the «Heart-Clearing Remedy» Not to Be Discarded

### Effects of the Lotus Seed Core

- ✅ **Clears Heart fire** — for vexation, mouth and tongue sores
- ✅ **Lowers blood pressure** — contains **liensinine (莲心碱)**
- ✅ **Anti-arrhythmic** — contains **methylliensinine (甲基莲心碱)**
- ✅ **Antibacterial** — contains **flavonoids**

### Lotus Seed Core Tea

- **Method:** **3 g lotus seed core + 3 g licorice**, **steeped in boiling water**, **covered 5 minutes**
- **Dosage:** **3-5 g daily**
- **Note:** **those with stomach cold or diarrhea** should use with caution
